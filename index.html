What makes our coffee special!

There is more to speciality coffee than the looks of it. If there is one great thing about it,that is its quality. If you visit a coffee shop and see a good, experienced barista do their job, you will be amazed. You will notice the barista weigh the coffee beans on a scale, grind them, and pour them with a unique technique. Seeing how a barista does the preparation is one thing; having the actual aroma and taste of the speciality coffee is another. You will savour something like no other with it's rich flavour notes and strong aroma, no matter what your order is. This is because the CoffeePot pays particular attention to the quality of the coffee beans, the use of proper tools and machines, and the right preparation techniques.
